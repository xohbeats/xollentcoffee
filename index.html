Why Xollent?

Hey, I’m D’Andre Spears—but you might know me as XoH! Beats. I started Xollent Coffee right here in my creative space, the same studio where I produce music, write stories, and bring ideas to life. This brand was born during one of those long, late nights where inspiration strikes, but only if you’re locked in and fueled up.

Xollent Coffee is for us—the creators, the night owls, and everyone chasing something bigger. Every blend is thoughtfully chosen, small-batch roasted, and made with people like you in mind.

Thanks for trusting my brand. I’ve got you every step of the way.
— D’Andre / XoH! Beats ☕✨
